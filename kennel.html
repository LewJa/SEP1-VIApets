Kennel


With so many people staying home during the pandemic, the need for companionship grew, and pets became a lifeline for those facing isolation. But as life returned to normal and people went back to work, pet sales declined.That’s when we had an idea: rather than just selling pets, we could also offer a safe and caring kennel service. Now, pet owners can rely on us to look after their furry friends whenever they’re away, whether for vacations or business trips.
