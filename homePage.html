Our History

This business started as an pet shop where we only sold a few kinds of pets, but during the pandemic there was an increase in animal adoptions thanks to the lockdown, when people were stuck home.

Our Kennel System

After the pandemic started getting better a lot of pets were left alone at home since their owners had to leave to go to work or go on holidays. That's how we started our kennel system, where people can bring their pets and we can look after them.

VIAPets

VIA actually stands for "Very Important Animals". We are a group of animal lovers who want to help animals in need.
