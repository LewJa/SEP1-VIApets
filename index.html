Our History

The idea of ​​opening a pet store appeared even before the well-known pandemic. At that time, many people felt the need for a pet nearby; at the end of the pandemic, we decided to expand the business, and we opened a kennel so that professionals could take care of the animals at any time. In the process, we faced the fact that some people, for some individual reasons, can no longer take care of their pets, and that is why we can also take this pet and help find a new owner. Our Kennel System

Would you like someone to take care of your pet? Then, use our services and contact us; we will provide information about available places and create a reservation. All we need to know is the period you need to leave the pet in our kennel, some information about the pet, and register you as a new client if you still need to be our customer. Currently, the rate is 20 euros per day, regardless of the type of pet.

VIAPets
